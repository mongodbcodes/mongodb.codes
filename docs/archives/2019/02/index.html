How it works

1
Client send email
You automatically send copy to us. ZemCIMail convert email into markdown and generate new github issue making it available for github flow integration.
2
Issue commented by your team
ZemCIMail convert comment into html and send back to client via email under your email name. It is 2 way connection with client now.
3
Client reply on email
ZemCIMail convert email into markdown and add to related issue as a comment. All attachments will be listed and available for download by direct link.

Features

Email Tracking status

Each comment has delivery and open status. We update "open" status each time, when client opens email.

FROM: detection

We automatically detect your email address. All Comment notifications to client sent from issues looks like reply by Your email.

SPF & DKIM & DRAC

We support SPF, DKIM and DRAC. Your emails should not go to Junk.

Stop word

Not all comments should go to client. Use skip-notify to prevent comments from sending to client.
